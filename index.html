Introduction

Welcome to Diksha Computers

Diksha Computers offers a comprehensive series of skill-development courses designed to bridge the gap from college to career. Our courses, crafted under the brand "Diksha Computers," focus on empowering students with knowledge-based skills essential for career growth in various sectors. Diksha Computers provides certificate courses, with durations of 120 hours and 60 hours. The 120-hour courses are recognized and certified by Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University (YCMOU), ensuring industry-relevant credentials. Our courses cover a wide range of skills required for career success in the services sector. With a focus on employability and practical learning, we offer 32 courses across 12 diverse sectors: Accounting Back Office Career Readiness Designing Digital Arts Digital Freelancing Hardware & Networking Management New Collar Jobs Programming Enroll in Diksha Computers courses today to gain the skills needed to excel in your chosen career path!
